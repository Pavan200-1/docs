## **Getting Started :**

To Intergrate, create an account with Kiwiwall using the link below :

https://www.kiwiwall.com/register

After Successful Login into your Kiwiwall DashBoard, you will find the below page, then go to **Create App**

![Kiwi Wall 1 Pn](/images/kiwi_wall_1.png)

Create a new App by giving name, URL and all required details... **Postback Url :** Copy Correct Postback URL from your  **Admin Panel --\> Settings --\> Postbacks S2S --\> KiwiWall**

## Example

http://yoururl.com//postbacks/kiwiwall.php

![Kiwi Wall 2 Pn](/images/kiwi_wall_2.png)

Then your app will be created and shown like below...

![Kiwi Wall 3 Pn](/images/kiwi_wall_3.png)

Final Step is to Copy the **_Wall Id_, _Api Key_** and **_Secret Key_** and paste it in your `Admin Panel --> Settings --> OfferWalls --> KiwiWall`

<Check>
  Note : Copy and paste just the Wall Id from the iframe code like below
</Check>

![Kiwi Wall 4 Pn](/images/kiwi_wall_4.png)

**That's it..\!**
