## Getting Started

To Intergrate AdGem Offerwall, you’ll need the following:

- **AdGem Publisher Account**

  [https://www.droidoxy.com/item/adgem-offerwall](https://www.droidoxy.com/item/adgem-offerwall/)

## Installing AdGem Addon

- Log into your hosting space (server) via Cpanel or FTP client.
- In the extracted addon archive folder, find **_upload_** folder
- Upload the **_Contents of upload folder_** to your server.
- After uploading the files you should go to [http://www.your-domain.com/install](http://www.your-domain.com/**install)
- That's it. AdGem Offerwall will be installed and will show you a confirmation message.

## Configuring AdGem Offerwall

Please follow the below steps to configure AdGem Offerwall:

1. Register Adgem Account

   https://dashboard.adgem.com/register?utm_source=yash@yashdev.com&utm_medium=yashdev-web-site
2. Login into your **_AdGem DashBoard _**and Go to Apps

   ![Adgem 1 Pn](/images/adgem_1.png)
3. Create a **_New App_**.

   ![Adgem 2 Pn](/images/adgem_2.png)
4. Give your App Name and click on **My app is not currently on App Store.**

   ![Adgem 3 Pn](/images/adgem_3.png)
5. Fill up all the details and click on Create App.

   ![Adgem 4 Pn](/images/adgem_4.png)
6. Copy the **_App id_** and paste it on your admin panel `Settings -> offerwalls -> adgem page`.

   ![Adgem 5 Pn](/images/adgem_5.png)

   **That's it ..\!**
