## **Requirements :**

To Intergrate, create an account with Cpalead using the link below :

https://cpalead.com/get-started.php?ref=765337

After Successful Login into your Cpa Lead DashBoard, you will find the below page, then go to Offer Wall --\> Create Offer Wall

![Cpalead 1 Pn](/images/cpalead_1.png)

and then create a new Offer Wall by selecting a theme of your choice

![Cpalead 2 Pn](/images/cpalead_2.png)

and then give your App Details ... like below and save it ..

![Cpalead 3 Pn](/images/cpalead_3.png)

After Saving your App Details, Go to **Offer Wall --\> Manage** and click on **View Code button** of your App.

![Cpalead 4 Pn](/images/cpalead_4.png)

And then, copy the Direct URL and paste it in your **Admin Panel --\> Settings --\> OfferWalls --\> CpaLead**

![Cpalead 5 Pn](/images/cpalead_5.png)

copy the Direct URL and paste it in your **Admin Panel** --\> **Settings** --\> **OfferWalls** --\> **CpaLead **Final step : **Setting up Postback Url :** Copy Correct Postback url from your **Admin Panel **--\>**Settings ** --\>**Postbacks S2S ** --\>**CpaLead** 

## Example :

[http://yoururl.com/postbacks/cpalead.php?subid={subid}&subid2={subid2}&virtual_currency={virtual_currency}](http://yoururl.com/postbacks/cpalead.php?subid={subid}&subid2={subid2}&virtual\\\_currency={virtual\\\_currency})

And Paste it in your **CpaLead Dashboard** by going to **Postback --\> Configuration**

![Cpalead 6 Pn](/images/cpalead_6.png)

Paste your **postback url** in the field and the password is optional 

**​That's it.. \!**
