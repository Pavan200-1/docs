## **Getting Started :**

To Intergrate Cpalead offerwall, you’ll need the following:

1. Create an cpalead account

   https://cpalead.com/get-started.php?ref=765337
2. Postback URL for Cpalead Network
   - Login to Admin Interface
   - Navigate to `Settings -> Postbacks S2S` on the admin sidebar
   - Copy the Cpalead Postback URL as shown in the admin interface
   - The Postback URL may look something similar to

After Successful Login into your **_Cpa Lead DashBoard_**, you will find the below page, then go to `Offer Wall --> Create Offer Wall`

![Cpalead 1 Pn](/images/cpalead_1.png)

Create a **_New OfferWall_** by selecting a theme of your choice

![Cpalead 2 Pn](/images/cpalead_2.png)

Give your App Details ... like below and save it ..

![Cpalead 3 Pn](/images/cpalead_3.png)

After Saving your App Details, Go to `Offer Wall --> Manage` and click on View Code button of your App.

![Cpalead 4 Pn](/images/cpalead_4.png)

Copy the Direct URL and paste it in your `Admin Panel --> Settings --> OfferWalls --> CpaLead`**.**

![Cpalead 5 Pn](/images/cpalead_5.png)

## Example :

[http://yoururl.com/postbacks/cpalead.php?subid=&subid2=&virtual_currency=](http://yoururl.com/postbacks/cpalead.php?subid={subid}&subid2={subid2}&virtual_currency={virtual_currency})

Paste it in your CpaLead Dashboard by going to `Postback --> Configuration`

![Cpalead 6 Pn](/images/cpalead_6.png)

Paste your postback url in the field and the password is optional

**That's it.. \!**
