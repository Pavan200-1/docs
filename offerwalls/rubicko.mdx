## Requirements :

To Intergrate, create an account with Rubicko using the link below:

https://rubicko.com/dashboard/signup.php

Login into your Rubicko DashBoard, you will find the below page

![Rubicko 1 Jpe](/images/rubicko_1.jpeg)

Create a new offerwall 

![Rubicko 2 Pn](/images/rubicko_2.png)

Then, Navigate to **Offerwall --\> Create New** 

![Rubicko 3 Pn](/images/rubicko_3.png)

Fill the details and click on **Create Offerwall** button.

![Runciko 4 Pn](/images/runciko_4.png)

Once you created offerwall you will get **offerwall id** which is used to integrate offerwall into you app/website 

Save it and then go to **INTEGRATION TAB**

**Settings —\>offerwalls page **

**That’s it ..\!**
