## Getting Started :

To Intergrate Rubicko offerwall, you’ll need the following:

Rubicko Account

- Register with Rubicko platform.

  (https://rubicko.com/dashboard/signup.php)

![Rubicko 1 Jpe](/images/rubicko_1.jpeg)

## **Getting Rubicko Id's**

To Intergrate Rubicko offerwall, you’ll need the following Id's

- Publisher ID
- Offerwall ID
- Secret ID

Please follow the below steps to get the required Id's

1. Login into your **_Rubicko Dashboard_**  and Click on  `offerwalls --> create new`

   ![Rubicko 3 Pn](/images/rubicko_3.png)
2. Fill out all the details and Click the  **_Create Offerwall_**  button.

   ![Runciko 4 Pn](/images/runciko_4.png)
3. Go to `offerwalls -> Rubicko offerwall` and copy the `Pub Id`, `offerwall Id` and  `Secret Id`

## **Integrating Offerwall**

1. Login to your Dashboard
2. Navigate to `Offerwalls --> All Offerwalls`
3. Select and `Edit Rubicko Offerwall` and scroll down to Id’s Section
4. Paste the copied `Pub Id`, `Offerwall Id` and `Secret id` and click on the **Save Changes** button to complete the integration

   ![Rubicko 5 Jpe](/images/rubicko_5.jpeg)
